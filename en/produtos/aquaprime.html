About the Technology

Reinventing primary wastewater treatment

The AquaPrime® applies cloth media filtration technology — already proven in tertiary treatment — to revolutionize the primary stage. By combining innovative filtration with chemical coagulation/flocculation, AquaPrime® achieves TSS and BOD removals far superior to conventional primary clarifiers.

The result is a significantly lower organic load for subsequent biological stages, enabling existing systems to be right-sized or expanded without extensive civil works. Especially valuable for WWTP retrofits and expansions with space and budget constraints.

Replaces Primary Clarifiers

Occupies a fraction of the space of a conventional clarifier with much higher TSS and BOD removal.

Reduced Biological Load

Lower organic load at the biological reactor inlet allows for expanded capacity or reduced reactor volumes.

Lower CAPEX

Fewer civil works and smaller required area translate into significant savings in total project capital cost.

AquaPrime® — replacing conventional primary clarifiers
Operating Principle

Primary filtration with integrated chemical dosing

Raw influent undergoes coagulant and flocculant dosing before entering the AquaPrime® modules. Coagulation/flocculation forms larger, denser flocs that are then captured by the cloth media on the rotating discs.

Continuous operation with automatic backwash keeps the discs operational even with high influent solids concentrations. The generated primary sludge is discharged in a concentrated form, reducing the volume to be treated and improving digestion efficiency.

1. Coagulation/Flocculation

Upstream chemical dosing forms flocs that greatly improve capture efficiency in the cloth media.

2. Primary Filtration

Cloth media discs capture solids, colloids, and flocs at the WWTP inlet stage.

3. Concentrated Sludge

Removed solids are discharged in concentrated form, optimizing sludge treatment.
