Sludge Thickening

Sludge thickening equipment for municipal and industrial WWTPs — gravity belt thickeners and dissolved air flotation units to increase dry solids content before dewatering or digestion.

DS output ≥ 4–6% Capture > 95% solids SS 304L / 316L
2 technologies available
  • OMEGA Series GBT — Gravity Belt Thickener OMEGA SD · MD · LD · THC — up to 200 m³/h
  • DAF — Dissolved Air Flotation ALPHA · DELTA · GAMMA — up to 650 m³/h

Sludge Dewatering

Mechanical dewatering and thermal drying solutions — reducing sludge volume and mass to simplify transport, final disposal, and energy recovery at municipal and industrial WWTPs.

DS output up to 92% Volume reduction –80% Continuous operation
3 technologies available
  • OMEGA Series Belt Filter Press OMEGA 100000 · NHP · CC — up to 28% DS
  • Screw Press Silent, compact, low energy consumption
  • Low Temp. Sludge Dryer EAHD · EAWD — up to 92% DS / <70°C
